About This Home

Beautiful 2500 square foot home within walking distance to the 1.1 billion dollar remodeled Westfield shopping center and Santana Row. 5 bedrooms with 3.5 luxurious bathrooms. Expansive oak wide plank engineered flooring throughout main living areas. 10 foot ceilings in the entire home. Chef inspired kitchen with oversized island, 36" Thermador stainless steel range and built-in Lebeihr refrigerator. Once you walk through the double front entry doors you encounter the living room for relaxed entertaining. Further down the hall will bring you to the open kitchen and family/dining areas. The master retreat has access to the rear yard for morning meditations and winding down in the evening on the back patio. Walking distance to Valley Fair and Santana Row and biking distance to the new Apple campus, the home is centrally located to world class shopping, restaurants, nightlife and entertainment. Close to Highways 280 and 880 for an easy commute.
