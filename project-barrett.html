About This Home

Villa Cambrian Neighborhood! Beautiful remodeled 2125 SF w/ open concept & high end finishes that todays buyers are craving. Originally built in 1960 & totally renovated in 2018.The wide entry area has 2 skylights bringing in natural light. Nice conversation area as soon as you enter the home with space to read, study or gather in a more intimate space. Kitchen features Carrara Marble, modern white shaker cabinets & large island w/ top of the line appliances. The open DR & LR have doors to access the yard. Laundry Room. This single story home also has a 2 car attached garage & large storage shed in the backyard. Oster OR Carlton ES, Dartmouth OR Union MS & Leigh HS. Close to Cambrian Park Plaza Shopping Center, Camden Community Center, Houge Park, Schools, easy access to 85 & 17 & so much more.
