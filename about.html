Insight into the Company

Handa Developers Group, is a Silicon Valley based technology driven Real Estate Development Firm that focuses on maximizing the investors & customers dollars. We have developed an unparalleled approach to construction which allows us to complete every project within a set timeframe and budget.


Our founder, Nitin Handa, began his mission over 10 years ago to revolutionize & capitalize Real Estate Development by focussing on four core areas of the industry: acquisition/diligence, capital formation, construction/operations, and sales.


Marlin Home Solutions, LLC (the “Fund”) was formed for the purpose of acquiring and managing residential and commercial class real estate assets. The Company will seek to acquire, manage, and sell high quality real estate assets with the intention of providing participating investors with a real estate focused investment opportunity that combines income, principal investment growth, and elements of capital preservation. The Fund is managed by Handa Developers Group (“HDG” or the “Fund Manager”), a California based company. HDG is managed by two highly experienced real estate and finance professionals with a combined 20 years of experience in the real estate business.
