About This Home

Beautifully remodeled home on prestigious Hilmar Street of Santa Clara. Open concept with lots of nature light, loaded w/ top quality finishes. Gourmet Kitchen feautures large kitchen island & high end appliances (Thermador & Viking). This house has a new permitted In-law unit in the back with its own kitchen, bedroom & bathroom which is perfect for Airbnb. The main house is 1993 and the Granny unit is 414 for a total living space of 2407sf. Live in the main house and cover big part of your loan by renting out the secondary unit. The home is almost like a new construction with new AC, Stucco, Plumbing, Electrical, Paint, Kitchen, Bathrooms, and the list goes on. Engineered flooring & tile floors in bathrooms. Master bedroom has LG walk-in closet & amazing tile work in the master bathroom along with double sinks & large walk in shower. Incredible neighborhood and tree-lined street close to shopping, University and Tech companies
